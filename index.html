    Welcome to Restaurant Barr

    New traditions from the North Sea region

    Barr is an informal restaurant with an attached bar. Northern European dishes such as salted waffles, schnitzel and meatballs are served, as well as a selection of traditional types of beer, akvavit and wine. Barr's menu conveys the essence of what we have chosen to call North Sea cuisine.

    See menu →

    Thorsten Schmidt

    Thorsten, who is a former chef and owner of restaurant Malling & Schmidt in Aarhus, recently upped his tent poles and moved to Copenhagen to explore the unpretentious but inviting dishes he remembers from his childhood. He grew up on the border between Denmark and Germany, and growing up in Northern Europe forms the background for the atmosphere he brings to restaurant Barr.
